Project 1: Portfolio Website | September 2025 - October 2025

Project 2: Academic Oasis | March 2023 - April 2025

One of the most valuable parts of university is the opportunity it unlocks for students. The Academic Oasis project was a website dedicated to helping students find opportunities that exist within Ontario Tech. The project inevitably got put on pause, but it was an amazing experience nonetheless.

Project 3: Clubs & Societies Budget Sheet | March 2023 - April 2023

One of the challenges most Clubs and Societies face, is having an idea of their budget, as they process multiple transactions every week. Using Google Sheets, I was able to build a mockup budget sheet template for club and society executives to track their transactions.

Project 4: Instagram Re-design | February 2023 - March 2023

One of the projects I undertook was re-designing our company's social media. The challenge was that the instagram had been neglected and we had irrelevant photos posted on the page. I used canva to design and instagram to post the newly created content.

Project 5: Artillery Coordinate Calculator | June 2021 - July 2021

When I was younger I used to play roblox, and there was a military game on the platform where you could drive artillery vehicles. I created a calculator to use the pythagorean theorum using the grid squares to figure out the distance to my enemy targets.
